5 Interesting Facts About Arizona

  1. Arizona became a state in 1912 and was the 48th state.
  2. Arizona is one of only two states that don't observe daylight savings.
  3. Arizona is the top producer of copper in the U.S.
  4. Arizona temperatures rise above 100 degrees for over 100 days per year.
  5. It is unlawful to refuse a glass of water to someone in Arizona.
