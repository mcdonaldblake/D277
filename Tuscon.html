5 Interesting Facts About Tuscon (Verdugo, 2024)

  1. Tucson’s name originates from a Tohono O’odham word
  2. Tucson is one of the few places where students get time off for a rodeo.
  3. Tucson was home to the first skateboard park.
  4. El Charro is the oldest Mexican restaurant in the country continuously run by the same family.
  5. Buffalo Exchange was founded in Tucson in 1974.
